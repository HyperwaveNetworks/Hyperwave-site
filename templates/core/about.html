WHO WE ARE

High-Speed Internet Solutions

We understand that reliable internet is the backbone of modern operations. That's why we offer:

Fiber Optic Connectivity

Enterprise-grade symmetrical connections with speeds up to 1Gbps, perfect for businesses requiring uninterrupted cloud access, VoIP services, and large data transfers.

Wireless Broadband

Flexible wireless solutions providing stable connectivity even in areas without fiber infrastructure, ideal for remote offices and residential areas.

Advanced Security Systems

We provide complete security ecosystems to protect your people, assets, and data:

  • Smart Surveillance: HD CCTV systems with AI analytics, cloud storage, and remote monitoring capabilities
  • Access Control: Biometric and RFID-based systems for secure entry management
  • Perimeter Protection: Electric fences, automated gates, and razor wire installations
  • Cybersecurity: Enterprise-grade firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about our services and operations

What areas do you currently service?

We provide services throughout Nairobi and surrounding areas including Utawala, Ruai, Githunguri, Ngara, Park Road, and more. Contact us to confirm service availability in your location.

What internet speeds can you provide?

We offer speeds from 5 Mbps to 1 Gbps with symmetrical upload/download on fiber connections. Our solutions are tailored to your business requirements.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, M-Pesa, credit cards, and checks with flexible payment schedules for larger projects and discounted rates for quarterly/annual plans.

Do you offer maintenance for existing systems?

Yes, we offer customizable maintenance packages for both our systems and those installed by others, including monthly, quarterly, or annual schedules and 24/7 monitoring.

What is your typical response time?

4 hours for critical issues and 24 hours for non-critical matters, with priority response for premium support clients and 24/7 emergency support for mission-critical systems.

Can you work with our existing IT team?

Absolutely. We collaborate effectively with in-house IT teams, providing specialized services, training, and clear communication channels for ongoing support.

What security systems do you offer?

We provide HD CCTV, access control (biometric, PIN, RFID), electric fencing, alarm systems, and integrated security platforms with remote monitoring capabilities.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes, initial consultations are free for most services, with detailed proposals including timelines and pricing to help you make informed decisions.
